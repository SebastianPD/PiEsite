What we do

Make Games

If you're a trailblazer you come up with an idea for a game and make a prototype. Once that is over you pitch it to other members and they choose if they want to be part of your project.

Play Games

We test games made by the people in the club and we also play games that are professionally made. This helps with the creative process.

Talk About Games

On occasion we get speakers from the video game industry like Jackbox, Iron Galaxy, Raw Trills and more to give their insight and experience in the industry.

Go to our meetings!

You will meet pleanty of people passionate about video games and maybe you can help publish one.
